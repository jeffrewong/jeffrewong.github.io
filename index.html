Pete's Quote

The goal of this project was to create a life insurance website to fit the specifications and needs of a life insurance broker. The website was designed with another intern using HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP, and WordPress. The website was designed with a focus on attracting visitors of all ages towards buying a life insurance policy. The website was also designed to have a welcoming feel and to also be mobile-friendly.

  • Date Completed: August 2017
  • Created for: Masu Internet
  • Link to Pete's Quote

STUF United

The goal of this project was to modernize the website of a non-profit organization to something that is more visually appealing to both the younger and older generation. The website was designed with a group of interns using HTML, CSS, and WordPress. The website was designed with the primary objective of user retention and attraction but also the secondary objective of promoting donations and subscriptions to newsletters.

  • Date Completed: July 2017
  • Created for: Masu Internet
